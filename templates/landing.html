Features

Everything You Need in One Place

From face-scan attendance to automated salary slips — ScanBite handles it all.

QR Attendance

Employees scan a QR code to open the attendance portal. No app install needed — works on any phone browser.

Face Recognition

AWS Rekognition verifies employee identity in real-time. Prevents buddy punching with 90%+ accuracy.

Salary Automation

Auto-calculate salary from attendance. Generate PDF salary slips with one click — fixed or per-day mode.

Meal Management

Employees confirm meals via QR. Canteen gets real-time headcount. Reduce food waste with accurate planning.

Shift Management

Configure shifts with start/end time, grace period, and half/full day thresholds. Auto late-mark on check-in.

Employee Portal

Self-registration via QR link. Employees submit their own details and face photo — no manual data entry.

Reports & Analytics

Filter attendance by date, employee, status. Export data. View present/absent/late trends at a glance.

Multi-Role Access

Separate dashboards for Admin, Company, and Canteen. Each role sees only what they need — secure by design.
